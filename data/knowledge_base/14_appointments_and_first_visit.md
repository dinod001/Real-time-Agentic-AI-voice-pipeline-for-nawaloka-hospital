# Nawaloka Hospital — Appointments & Your First Visit

## 1. Booking an Appointment

You can book a consultant (channelling) appointment in three ways:

- **Through the assistant** — say the department or doctor and a date and time, for example "book a dermatology appointment tomorrow at 11 am" or "book Dr. Kusal Fernando next Monday at 10 am". If you are not sure who to see, ask for the doctors available in a department and the assistant will list them.
- **By phone** — call the main line on +94 11 544 4444.
- **At the OPD desk** — in person at the hospital.

To **reschedule**, ask to move your existing appointment to a new time. To **cancel**, ask to cancel your appointment. You do not need to remember a booking number — the assistant can find your upcoming appointment for you.

## 2. What to Bring for Your First Appointment

Please bring the following to your first visit:

- **A photo ID** — your National Identity Card (NIC), passport, or driving licence.
- **Your phone** — registration uses an OTP (one-time password) sent by SMS for verification.
- **Any past medical records, reports, or scans** you already have that relate to your problem.
- **A list of your current medications** (including doses and any over-the-counter products or supplements).
- **Your insurance card or details**, if you intend to claim.

First-visit registration takes about 10 minutes. Please arrive about 15 minutes before your appointment time so registration is complete before your consultation.

## 3. On the Day

- Check in at the OPD reception or the relevant department desk and confirm your details.
- If you booked a specialist (channelling) appointment, your slot is with a specific consultant; waiting times can vary if a consultant is delayed by an inpatient emergency.
- Bring any creams, inhalers, or devices relevant to your condition so the doctor can see exactly what you are using.

## 4. Cancellations and No-Shows

If you cannot attend, please cancel or reschedule in advance so the slot can be offered to another patient. Repeated no-shows for channelling appointments may affect future priority booking. There is no cancellation charge for cancelling ahead of time.
