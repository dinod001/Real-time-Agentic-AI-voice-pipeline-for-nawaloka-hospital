# Nawaloka Hospital — Opening Hours (What Time Does the Hospital Open?)

## 1. Hospital Opening Hours

**What time does Nawaloka Hospital open? What are the hospital's opening hours and OPD timings?**

The Nawaloka Hospital main facility operates 24 hours daily (00:00–24:00) with emergency care available around the clock. The Outpatient Department (OPD) operates 15 hours daily, from 07:00 to 22:00 (the hospital OPD opens at 7:00 am and closes at 10:00 pm). Specialist consultant channelling clinics operate by appointment, typically 14:00–21:00.

Hospital opening hours summary: the hospital opens at 07:00 for outpatients and operates 24 hours for emergencies. What time does the hospital open — 7:00 am (07:00) for the OPD, and 24 hours every day for the Emergency Department.

The following services run around the clock, 24 hours, every day including weekends and public holidays:

- **Emergency Department (ETU)** — 24 hours, every day.
- **Inpatient wards and ICU/CCU** — 24 hours.
- **Labour room and maternity** — 24 hours.
- **Pharmacy (main / emergency)** — 24 hours.
- **Laboratory and emergency radiology** — 24 hours.

### 1.1 Outpatient (OPD) Clinic Hours

- **General OPD clinics** operate **07:00–22:00** daily.
- **Specialist consultant (channelling) clinics** run **by appointment**, typically in the **14:00–21:00** band. Exact slots depend on each consultant's schedule and are held in the appointment system — ask the assistant for a specific doctor's availability.

So, if someone asks "what time does the hospital open" or "what are your opening hours": the hospital itself never closes (24-hour emergency care), the general outpatient clinics run from 7 am to 10 pm, and specialist consultations are by appointment, mostly in the afternoon and evening.

## 2. Visiting Hours

General ward visiting hours are **11:00–13:00 and 17:00–20:00** daily. Intensive care units (ICU/CCU) have restricted visiting (short windows, usually two visitors at a time, no children under 12). Maternity ward visiting is generally **11:00–13:00 and 17:00–19:00**. Please check with the ward nurse, as visiting may be adjusted for infection-control reasons.
